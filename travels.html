Around Oaxaca: Starting from the Instituto Cultural de Oaxaca, A steep route that ends at a tall hill overlooking the city

From Oaxaca to Tule: A long, but flat run to Tule (home of one of the oldest trees in the world!) and back to Oaxaca

Chile 2012:

My favorite running route to see Santiago, Chile

We took a 2 day trip to see valparaiso and viña del mar. I recommend the excellent Tours for Tips program. I enjoyed running along the winding streets, and visiting Quinta Vergara in Viña del Mar

A weekend trip to Pucon included a memorable hike up Mt. Villarrica , hiking in Huerquehue National Park and a bike ride.

I also did the popular 4 day tour from San Pedro de Atacama to Uynui, Bolivia and back. Details here
